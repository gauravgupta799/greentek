Our Story

Ken Lorence founded GreenTek inspired by his observations of refrigerated trucks on the highway. He often thought about the enormous amount of fuel these trucks must be burning and wondered how he could make a difference. Motivated by a desire to reduce environmental impact and improve efficiency, Ken launched GreenTek.

He wanted to develop innovative, energy-efficient solutions for the cold chain industry, aiming to create a sustainable future that future generations would thank us for.

Meet our founder

Ken Lorence, founder and president of GreenTek Cold Chain Solutions, is a pioneer in sustainable refrigeration technology. With a passion for innovation and environmental responsibility, Ken has led GreenTek to success with groundbreaking solutions like the BioPCM® and ATTS.

Under his leadership, the company has completed over 75+ projects, significantly reducing fuel consumption and emissions for clients worldwide.
