Advanced BioPCM® Technology

Harnessing Plant-Based Phase Change Materials for Superior Energy Efficiency and Environmental Sustainability

What is BioPCM®?

BioPCM® is an innovative, plant-based phase change material designed to revolutionize thermal management across various industries. By absorbing and releasing large amounts of thermal energy at specific temperatures, BioPCM® offers unparalleled energy efficiency and sustainability.

Key Features of BioPCM®

100% Plant-Based
and Sustainable

BioPCM® is derived from renewable plant sources, making it an environmentally friendly alternative to traditional thermal management materials. It is USDA Certified Bio-Preferred, ensuring minimal environmental impact.

Wide Temperature
Range

BioPCM® is engineered to store and release thermal energy at precise temperatures, ranging from -75°C to 175°C. This flexibility makes it suitable for a wide array of applications, from cryogenic storage to room temperature environments.

Reliable and
Durable

BioPCM® has been rigorously tested to demonstrate consistent performance over thousands of thermal cycles. It is non-toxic, non-flammable, and maintains its efficacy over time, ensuring long-term reliability.

Applications of BioPCM®

Cold Chain Logistics

In refrigerated transport, BioPCM® is a core component of the Active Thermal Transport System (ATTS). It ensures consistent temperature control, reducing fuel consumption and enhancing the quality of transported goods.

Building Energy Efficiency

BioPCM® is used in products like the ENRG Blanket® and ENRG Panel® to enhance the thermal efficiency of buildings. By stabilizing indoor temperatures, these products reduce the need for HVAC systems, resulting in significant energy savings.

Data Centers and Telecom Shelters

BioPCM® helps maintain optimal operating temperatures in data centers and telecom shelters, reducing energy consumption and increasing the reliability of critical infrastructure.
